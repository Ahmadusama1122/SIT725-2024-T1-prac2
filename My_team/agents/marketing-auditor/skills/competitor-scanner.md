# Competitor Scanner Skill

When analyzing the competitive landscape:

## Competitor Identification

### Three Tiers
1. **Direct Competitors** — Same product/service, same market
2. **Indirect Competitors** — Different product, same problem solved
3. **Aspirational Competitors** — Market leaders to benchmark against

### Discovery Methods
- Search "[business type] + [location]" (for local businesses)
- Search "[product category] alternatives"
- Check review sites (G2, Capterra, Yelp, Google Reviews)
- Look at "compare" and "vs" pages
- Check industry directories

## Per-Competitor Analysis
For each competitor (aim for 3-5), collect:

1. **Website Quality** — Design, messaging, UX
2. **Pricing** — Plans, positioning (premium/value/mid-market)
3. **Content** — Blog frequency, quality, topics
4. **Social Proof** — Reviews count, ratings, testimonials
5. **Social Media** — Platforms active, follower counts, engagement
6. **Unique Positioning** — What do they claim is unique?
7. **Weaknesses** — What are they doing poorly?

## Opportunities
- Gaps no competitor is filling
- Weaknesses shared by all competitors
- Content topics competitors haven't covered
- Pricing gaps in the market
