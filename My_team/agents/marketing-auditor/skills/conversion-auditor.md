# Conversion Auditor Skill

When auditing conversion optimization:

## CTA Analysis (0-5 points)
- Primary CTA visible above the fold?
- CTA text is action-oriented (not "Submit" or "Click Here")?
- Contrasting button color?
- CTA appears at natural decision points?
- Only ONE primary CTA per page section?

## Form Optimization (0-5 points)
- Minimal form fields (name + email for lead gen)?
- Labels are clear?
- Error messages are helpful?
- Progressive disclosure for complex forms?
- Mobile-friendly input types?

## Trust Signals (0-5 points)
- Security badges on checkout/signup?
- Money-back guarantee visible?
- Privacy policy linked near forms?
- Real team photos (not stock)?
- Physical address/phone number present?

## Pricing Page (0-5 points)
- Clear plan comparison?
- Recommended plan highlighted?
- Annual vs monthly toggle?
- Feature comparison table?
- FAQ section addressing objections?

## Common Critical Findings
- Pricing inconsistencies across pages (check all mentions of prices)
- Broken CTAs or forms
- No clear next step after landing on the page
- Missing mobile responsiveness
- Slow page load blocking conversions

## Output
Score: X/20 for Conversion Optimization
List all findings with severity and specific fix recommendations
