# Website Analyzer Skill

When analyzing a website's content and messaging:

## Discovery Steps
1. Fetch the homepage and extract:
   - Main headline and subheadline
   - Value proposition
   - Navigation structure
   - Footer links (sitemap discovery)
2. Identify and fetch key pages: /pricing, /about, /blog, /features, /contact
3. Detect business type: SaaS, e-commerce, local business, agency, creator, course

## Content Analysis Checklist

### Value Proposition (0-5 points)
- [ ] Clear in under 10 words?
- [ ] Above the fold on homepage?
- [ ] Addresses a specific pain point?
- [ ] Differentiates from competitors?
- [ ] Consistent across pages?

### Headlines (0-5 points)
- [ ] Compelling and specific?
- [ ] Benefit-oriented (not feature-oriented)?
- [ ] Appropriate length for context?
- [ ] Use power words?
- [ ] Match page intent?

### Copy Quality (0-5 points)
- [ ] Readable (short paragraphs, bullet points)?
- [ ] Free of jargon and filler?
- [ ] Uses active voice?
- [ ] Includes data/proof points?
- [ ] Appropriate tone for audience?

### Social Proof (0-5 points)
- [ ] Customer testimonials present?
- [ ] Case studies or results?
- [ ] Trust badges (awards, certifications, press)?
- [ ] Customer logos?
- [ ] Review scores (G2, Trustpilot, Google)?

## Output
Score: X/20 for Content & Messaging
Findings: List with severity (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
Recommendations: Specific, actionable improvements
