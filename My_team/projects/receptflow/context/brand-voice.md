# Receptflow Brand Voice Guide

## Core Identity
Receptflow is an AI receptionist for small businesses—dental, legal, trades, real estate, and more. We're your friendly business advisor who happens to be powered by AI, not a corporate tech company.

## Tone & Personality
- **Friendly & Professional**: Like talking to a helpful business mate who knows their stuff
- **Straightforward**: No fluff, no jargon, just what you need to know
- **Conversational**: Reads like a person talking, not a manual
- **Naturally Australian**: Uses Aussie slang where it fits ("Legend!", "No worries!", "Give us a go")
- **Results-Focused**: Everything circles back to outcomes—leads captured, appointments booked, money saved

## What We Say vs. What We Don't Say

### DO Say:
- "Answers like a human"
- "Never miss another lead"
- "Works with your calendar"
- "Live in 15 minutes"
- "AI receptionist"
- "Small business"
- Specific numbers and outcomes: "Capture 40 extra leads per month" or "Save 8 hours/week"
- Real pain points: "Missing calls costs you $8,000 per lead"

### DON'T Say:
- "AI-powered natural language processing"
- "Revolutionary game-changer"
- "Leverage synergies"
- "Virtual assistant" or "chatbot"
- "SMB" or "enterprise"
- Vague claims: "Improve efficiency" without specifics
- Corporate jargon
- Technical AI terminology

## Message Pillars

### 1. Never Miss a Lead
Your phone is ringing when you're on site, in a meeting, or closed. Every missed call is money lost. Receptflow answers 24/7, captures the details, and gets that lead to you instantly.

### 2. Sounds Like a Real Person
Not a robotic voice. Not a menu tree. Receptflow talks like a helpful person on your team. Your clients feel heard.

### 3. Works With Your Business
Whether you book appointments, take messages, or qualify leads—Receptflow learns how you work. It knows your calendar, your services, your team.

### 4. Set It Up in 15 Minutes
No IT team needed. No complicated integrations (though we've got them if you want). Connect your phone, pick your industry template, go.

### 5. Know Your Leads
Hot leads get flagged. Warm leads get queued. Cold leads get handled. Built-in lead scoring tells you where to focus.
